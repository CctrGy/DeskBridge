# DeskBridge USB protocol EVT codes

Format:
[EVT] 0xNNNN EVENT_NAME detail

0x1001 BATTERY_IS_LOW
  Source: chip_core power manager, pending integration.
  Meaning: battery level is below the safe threshold.

0x1002 AMP_SUPPLY_OVERHEAD
  Source: chip_core power manager, pending integration.
  Meaning: measured current is above the configured safe operating budget.

0x1003 POWER_EXTERNAL_DISABLED
  Source: chip_core power manager, pending integration.
  Meaning: external supply path was disabled or lost.

0x1004 USB_VBUS_PRESENT
  Source: chip_core/src/otros/usb/DeskUSB.cpp
  Meaning: USB VBUS is detected.

0x1005 USB_VBUS_LOST
  Source: chip_core/src/otros/usb/DeskUSB.cpp
  Meaning: USB VBUS is not detected.

0x1101 KEYPAD_BUTTON_EDGE
  Source: chip_core/src/otros/bus/KeypadPeripheral.cpp
  Meaning: CHIP_KEYPAD reported a button edge.
