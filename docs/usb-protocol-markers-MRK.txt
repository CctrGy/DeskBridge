# DeskBridge USB protocol MRK codes

Format:
[MRK] 0xNNNN marker.name

0xA001 shell.begin
  Source: chip_core/lib/shell/libs.cpp
  Meaning: USB shell was initialized and is ready to receive commands.

0xA100 usb.descriptor.self_powered_remote_wakeup
  Source: chip_core/include/usb/usb_descriptors.h
  Meaning: descriptor declares self-powered device with remote wakeup support.

0xA200 keypad.protocol.shared_library
  Source: shared_lib/DeskBridgeKeypadProtocol/src/KeypadProtocol.h
  Meaning: shared CORE<->KEYPAD protocol constants are in use.
